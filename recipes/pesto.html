Pasta al Pesto

Description:

Pasta with pesto is a classic first course prepared with basil pesto.

A delicious and fragrant condiment, basil pesto can be prepared in five minutes and with very few ingredients!

Ingredients:

  • 320g pasta
  • 150g basil
  • 40g grated parmesan
  • 25g pine nuts
  • extra vergin olive oil
  • salt

Steps:

  1. To prepare the pasta with pesto, immediately heat the water to cook the pasta.
  2. Meanwhile, wash and dry the basil well with a clean cloth.
  3. We put the basil in the blender bowl, add the oil, salt, garlic and pine nuts.
  4. Blend intermittently for a few seconds. This way the pesto will not heat up too much, maintaining its bright green color.
  5. We then add the grated cheese and blend again intermittently until we obtain the right consistency.
  6. Drain the pasta al dente and season it with plenty of pesto. Our pesto pasta is ready! Sprinkle it with grated cheese and decorate with two basil leaves before serving.
